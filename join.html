Join Our Team

We believe that being able to make science clear and engaging empowers us to solve problems in any field.

We believe that making science communication more prevalent helps build a culture where we can be fully ourselves—no more pressure to conform to labels like "STEM person" or "humanities person."

Yet, science communication is rarely mentioned in classrooms. That's why we run events and create online resources to help high schoolers learn it.

As a member of our tight-knit team, you'll be able to make a significant impact on students and our organization. We strive to communicate authentically and support each other's growth.

We review applications on a rolling basis and will follow up with you within one week of receiving your application.

Open Positions

We're currently not recruiting for specific roles. However, we always love to hear from anyone interested—send a message to team@scienceandus.org!

Other Ways to Get Involved

If you're in greater Boston and are in college or above, you can sign up to volunteer at our Makeathon on January 25. As a volunteer, you'll be a floating mentor, helping students with brainstorming, creating their projects, and/or researching STEM topics, and overall being a supportive role model.
