Net neutrality, environmental pollution, social media privacy—all those issues involve science, and affect much more than just scientists.

Understanding the fundamentals of STEM is crucial, yet many view it as intimidating or irrelevant. Science communication—explaining complex topics in engaging, clear ways—can bridge this gap.

Science & Us enables STEM-oriented students to better explain their passions to non-experts, and teaches them about interdisciplinary careers, like science media and policy, that are rarely mentioned in school.
