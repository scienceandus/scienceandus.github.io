üìù Description

Students everywhere deserve the opportunity to discover, learn, and practice science communication. At our Makeathons, 50 high schoolers gather for a day to make projects (such as articles, podcasts, and artwork) about STEM topics of their choice. As an Event Organizer, you'll play a crucial role in making sure that our events run smoothly and provide extraordinary experiences for students, volunteers, and sponsors. You'll interact with students frequently, and work with other team members to continuously raise the bar for our unique events.

üî• What you'll do

  • Recruit, communicate with, and coordinate event volunteers (mentors, workshop leaders, panelists)
  • Help plan flexible, realistic schedules with meaningful, fun activities
  • Help research, budget for, and purchase prizes, food, marketing materials, and workshop/project materials
  • Help market events to high schoolers in greater Boston, especially those from underserved communities and not primarily interested in STEM
  • Be a leader in day-of logistics, including but not limited to check-in, opening and closing sessions, and introducing and assisting workshop leaders
  • Engage with event attendees both in large groups and individually
  • Actively partake in post-event activities: team reflections, assessing attendee feedback, sharing updates with sponsors, making improvements to future events
